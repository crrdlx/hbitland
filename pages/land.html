Land

HBITLAND consists of 100 squares on the home page. Each square is one plot of land.

By owning a plot of HBITLAND, you get:

  1. your spot
  2. your image shown
  3. a link to whatever you like
  4. the right to claim land in a future 3D/VR/metaverse world if we get there
  5. serious bragging rights for being one of the first 100 settlers in HBITLAND

This is HBITLAND, it is only purchased with Hivebits (HBIT). To get land, you need some HBIT. Fortunately, getting HBIT is easy and you can get it for free by "mining" it (which is also very, very easy).

How to get HBIT

See the @Hivebits About page. Summarized, there are only two things to do. (1) Get a Hive account and (2) reply with command like !HBIT. That's it. You'll mine and then own HBIT.
