Land

In v1.0, there are 100 plots of HBITLAND.

HBITLAND consists of 100 squares on the home page, with each square as one plot of land.

By owning a plot of HBITLAND, you get:

  1. your plot of land
  2. your image shown
  3. a link to whatever you like
  4. the right to claim land in a future 3D/VR/metaverse world, if we get there
  5. the right to sell your land to any buyer
  6. serious "OG" bragging rights for being one of the first 100 settlers in HBITLAND

This is HBITLAND, it can only be purchased with Hivebits (HBIT). To get land, you need some HBIT. Fortunately, getting HBIT is easy and you can get it for free by "mining" it (which is also very, very easy).

How to get HBIT

See the @Hivebits About page. Summarized, there are only two things to do.

  1. Get a Hive account.
  2. Reply to a post or comment by including a command like: !HBIT

That's it. You'll mine, receive, and then own HBIT.

Also, you can get more Hivebits (HBIT) on one of the open markets: Hive-Engine, TribalDex, or LeoDEX.

How to get HBIT land

To get HBIT land, follow these steps:

  1. Send HBIT...details TBD.
  2. Include in the memo the plot number you wish to purchase.
  3. Complete this form if you wish to include a custom image and link. If the form is not completed, that's fine. Your Hive avatar will be used as the image and your Hive profile will be used as the link.
  4. Allow some time. The process is not automatic and must be done manually.
